Red Queen by Victoria Aveyard

Genre: Teen, Fiction

Rating: 8.5/10

Summary:

Red Queen by Victoria Aveyard tells the story of 17 year old, Mare Barrow, who lives in a world where your status is decided on the color of your blood. Red bloods are commoners while silvers are royals who possess special powers, however is there a possibility a red blood can possess the powers of a silver?
